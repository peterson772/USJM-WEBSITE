About University of Saint Joseph Mbarara

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education that promotes intellectual, spiritual, and moral development of students for service to humanity. We aim to equip graduates with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal and professional success.

Our Vision

To be a leading private university in Uganda and the East African region, recognized for academic excellence, innovative research, and transformative education that produces ethical leaders and responsible citizens.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Social Responsibility
  • Diversity and Inclusion
